Account and payment

Login & Register

In the unlogged status, click the avatar in the upper left corner of the main page. And then, In the left menu, click the “Login” button or the user avatar area to enter the login page.
Users can also register or retrieve the password on this page. In addition, it also provides 3 kinds of third-party quick login.

Account information

After login, the left menu shows the user avatars, nicknames and the usage of Cloud.
Click the user avatar to enter the account information page. This page can customize the avatar, modify the nickname, modify the login password, bind the phone number, bind the email, bind third-party account and log out,Device Management,Purchase history,Redeem code,Sign out.

Subscription entry
After login your DWG FastView account, you could click “Upgrade Account” in the left menu, or click “Upgrade” button on the account information page to view advanced features, and then you could upgrade your acount to enjoy them.
