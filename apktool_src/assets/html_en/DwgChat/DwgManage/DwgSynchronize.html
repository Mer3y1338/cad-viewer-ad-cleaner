Drawing synchronization

Prerequisites

You need to have friends or projects (self-created projects and joined projects) and have uploaded drawings.

Synchronization Setting

[Step 1]
Click "Project" in the navigation to enter the project list.

[Step 2]
Click the "+" button on the top right corner of the project list page, and select "Project Settings" in the popup menu to enter the project settings page.

[Step 3]
In the "Auto Synchronization" item on the project settings page, you can choose whether to enable automatic synchronization (the default is "Enable").

Synchronous Operation

[Step 1]
Click "Project" to enter the project list, select a project.

[Step 2]
Go to the "Drawings Management" section of the project,click to view the details of one drawing and enter the drawing details page.

[Step 3]
Click "Synchronize" in the detail page to synchronize the drawings.

Notes

1. After the automatic synchronization setting is turned on, all drawings from the project will automatically synchronize with the cloud when they are closed and opened, as long as they have been annotated and modified.
2. After the automatic synchronization setting is turned off, all drawings from the project that have been annotated and modified will not be synchronized with the cloud when the drawings are closed and opened. Manual synchronization is required.
3、This setting does not affect the synchronization of annotation modification in DwgChat, the modification of annotations in DwgChat is always automatically synchronized in real time.
4. Users can view the details of the drawings through the Homepage, Cloud List, Local Drawing List, and Project List. Clicking "Synchronize" on the details page can synchronize the drawings.
