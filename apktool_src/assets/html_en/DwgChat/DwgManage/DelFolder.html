Delete Folder

Prerequisites

You need to have a friend or a project (self-created project).

Delete Folder

In the drawing management of the project, click "More" on the right side of the folder or long press the folder to enter the details. Click "Delete" in the folder details to delete it.

Note:Only the project creator (person in charge) can delete the folder and after deletion, the folder cannot be recovered and will release the occupied cloud space.
