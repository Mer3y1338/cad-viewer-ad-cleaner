Move Folder

Prerequisites

You need to have friends or projects (self-created projects and joined projects) and have uploaded drawings.

Move Folder

In the drawing management of the project, click "More" on the right side of the folder or long press the folder to enter the details. Click "Move" in the folder details to move it.

Note:The Move folder is only available to the project creator (person in charge).
