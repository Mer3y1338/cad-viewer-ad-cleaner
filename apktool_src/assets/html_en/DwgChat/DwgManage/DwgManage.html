Drawing Management

Prerequisites

You need to have projects (self-created projects and joined projects) or friends.

Drawing Management

The drawings in the project are unified in Drawing Management, where you can view the details of the drawings and delete the drawings.

Click "Project" --> Select Project --> "Project Details" --> "Drawing Management" will be shown in the project details by default.

Note:The functions provided in Drawing Management are different for project owner and project members.
