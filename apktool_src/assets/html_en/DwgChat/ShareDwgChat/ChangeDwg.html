Change Drawing

Prerequisites

When you are in the ShareView mode, and you are the operator. Or you are in the MyView mode.

Change Drawing

In ShareView session, click "Change Drawing" icon on the top of the tool bar. You can change the drawing.

Note: The drawing you are going to change must be in the current project. If not, please upload it firstly.
