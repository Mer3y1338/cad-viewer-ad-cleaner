Enter DwgChat

Prerequisites

You need to have friends or projects (self-created projects and joined projects) and have sent drawings to the project or chat.

Enter DwgChat

You can enter DwgChat in the following ways:

1. Click the "DwgChat" button in the drawing information on the homepage.

On the homepage, view the details of the drawing, and click the "DwgChat" button on the drawing details page.

2. After opening the drawing, click the "DwgChat" icon on the top operation bar.

After opening the drawing, click the "DwgChat" icon, then select a project or friend to start "DwgChat".

3. Enter the "Message" interface, then click "DwgChat" icon above the input box.

Click "Project", choose one project then click the "Message" on the top navigation, you could see a "DwgChat" icon above the text input box, then click it.

4. Click the information bar in "Message" interface.

Click "Project", choose one project then click the "Message" on the top navigation, A blue information bar will appear at the top of the interface. Press "Click to enter".

Note:Only some one has entered the DwgChat, the blue information bar will appear at the top of the interface.

5. In the Message log, click the text link of "XXX has entered the DwgChat, click to enter"

Click "Project", choose one project then click the "Message" on the top navigation, enter DwgChat in the Message log interface.

Note:Every time a user enters a DwgChat, a record of entering will be generated in the chat history.

6. Click "DwgChat" on the drawing information page of a certain drawing in the project drawing management.

View the details of the drawing through the homepage, cloud map list, local map list, and project list. Click "DwgChat" on the details page.
