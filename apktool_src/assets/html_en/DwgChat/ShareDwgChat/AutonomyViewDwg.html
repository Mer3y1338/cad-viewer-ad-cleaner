MyView

Prerequisites

You need to have friends or projects (created by yourself and joined projects)

MyView

After switching to MyView mode, will not be affected by the actions of the operator. They can change and edit drawings freely. All the operations will not affect other users.

Click "DwgcChat" firstly, then switch to "MyView".

Note: MyView Members are not affected by the actions of the operator, and do not display the actions of the operator synchronously. They can view images freely. To become an operator, they need to switch to ShareView member firstly.
