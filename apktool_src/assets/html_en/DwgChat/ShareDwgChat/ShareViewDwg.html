ShareView

Prerequisites

You need to have friends or projects (created by yourself and joined projects)

ShareView

The DwgChat function of CAD Viewer allows multiple people to communicate and discuss the same drawing, with one person operating the drawing viewport or annotating, and the other members synchronously and automatically operating.

Enter "DwgChat" firstly, then click "ShareView".

Note: There are two types of users in ShareView: Operator and ShareView Member.
Operator: There can only be one operator in a project or a private chat at the same time. Operator can operate any drawings and all operations will be synchronized in the interface of ShareView members.
ShareView member: ShareView member can't do anything with the drawing, they can only wait for the Operator's actions and synchronize the display of their operations. ShareView members can become Operators by switching identities.
