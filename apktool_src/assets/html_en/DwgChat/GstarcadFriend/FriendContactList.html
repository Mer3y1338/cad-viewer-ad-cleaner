Contacts List

Click "Project", and press the "Contacts" icon on the top right corner. All your current friends will be shown.

Note: The list shows all your friends. If you want to add more friends please check Search Add Friends and Add Address Book Friends. In particular, friends with corporate membership benefits will have blue corner markers on their avatars. Dark blue are colleagues of this enterprise.
