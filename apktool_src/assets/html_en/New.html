New

Tap“+” button on the bottom right corner of the home page to display New and PDF to CAD.

1. New

You could create a new drawing by tapping “New”.
After drawing and editing in the new created drawing, you could tap “Save” on the top left and select saving location(you could tap “New Folder” to create a new folder if necessary).

2. PDF to CAD

Operation Steps:
Step 1: open a PDF file;
Step 2: click “PDF to CAD” button on the bottom; it will upload and convert the PDF file automatically;
Step 3: view conversion progress by clicking the icon on the top right of the PDF page; or selecting the PDF to CAD option in the Home page by clicking +(on the right bottom of Home page). The conversion results are saved at the following directory: My Cloud > PDF to CAD.
