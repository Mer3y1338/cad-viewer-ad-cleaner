Ellipse

Draw → Ellipse

Operations:

Step 1: click the “Draw” button and select “Ellipse” command;

Step 2: specify the first point.

Step 3: specify the second endpoint, and click “✓”on the panel to the next step.

Step 4: specify the half length of the second axis.

Step 4: click “✓” to finish it.
