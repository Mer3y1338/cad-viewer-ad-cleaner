Image

Annotation → Image   

Operation steps:

Step 1: in annotation list, choose “Image” command;

Step 2: specify the point to local Image annotation;

Step 3: you could select images from Album or take a photo instead. You can also take a photo with watermark to display some information you like. The number of images should be less than 10;

Step 4: in the “Image List” page, you could view selected images; add more images by tapping “+” button; replace the image by tapping the icon on the left bottom; add remark by tapping on image.

Step 5: after adding images tap on “OK” to finish.
