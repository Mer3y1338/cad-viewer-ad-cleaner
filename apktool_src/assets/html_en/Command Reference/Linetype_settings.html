Linetype settings:set linetype for new drawing objects.

Switch the current linetype
Operations:
Step 1: click “Linetype Settings” in Settings in Edit Mode;
Step 2: choose required linetype in the list;
Step 3: click “OK” .

Add linetypes to current from the linetype file in App
Operations:
Step 1: click “Linetype Settings” in Settings in Edit Mode;
Step 2: click “+” button on the top right of dialog box;
Step 3: choose required linetypes and add them to current drawing;
Step 4: click “OK” .

Add linetypes to current from the linetype file out of App
Operations:
Step 1: click “Linetype Settings” in Settings in Edit Mode;
Step 2: click “+” button on the top right of dialog box;
Step 3: click “Select a linetype file” button at the bottom of dialog box;
Step 3: bowser the linetype file in your device and select it;
Step 4: click “OK” .
